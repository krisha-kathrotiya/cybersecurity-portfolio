# Categories of Controls

There are three main categories of controls in the cybersecurity.

## 1. Administrative Control
 is a type of control which handles the human resources part of an organization. Policies and procedures that defines the employee responsibilities, data-handling and governance.

 ## 2. Technical Control
 defines technology-based safeguards such as firewalls, IDS, encryption, antivirus software, and access controls.

## 3. Physical/Operational Control
Controls that restrict physical access to assets, including locks, CCTV, and environmental protections.

## Types of these Controls

 - Preventative: Prevent the risk or attack from happening
 - Detective: Detects the attack that is in progress or had happened
 - Corrective: Restore systems after an incident
 - Deterrent: Discourage malicious activity
